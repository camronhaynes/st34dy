SQUAT CUES

  • Brace the cylinder: Big breath into belt line. Protects spine WITHOUT clenching PF.
  • Spread the floor: Feet screw outward. Activates glutes.
  • Chest over hips: No tipping forward. Lumbar curve intact.
  • Tripod foot: Big toe, pinky toe, center heel. All three.
  • Drive through heels: Back half of foot on the way up.
  • Grab the floor: Spread toes and grip. Fires the arch.
  • Release at top: Lockout, exhale, let the floor drop.

WHY FEET MATTER

Flat feet = no arch = posterior chain has no foundation. Deep Front Line: foot arches → inner calves → adductors → psoas → diaphragm → jaw. PF sits in the middle.
